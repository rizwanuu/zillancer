Where does the name "Zillancer" come from?

Zillancer is a combination of zillions and freelancers. The founder Husain Abdulla aims to connect zillions of opportunities to zillions of talents and grow an expert community of freelancers worldwide.

From the very beginning, Husain felt passionate about getting talented and skilled members to the platform, from a wide range of categories, including Microsoft Dynamics 365 consultants, SAP consultants, Oracle consultants, web developers, marketing specialists, graphic designers, and much more."Let's forget our limits and enter a world full of new possibilities."- Husain Abdulla. A World Of Limitless Possibilities.

At Zillancer, we believe there is a better way to do freelancing. A more valuable, less invasive way where clients can grow their business and save their costs. We are obsessively passionate about it, and our main idea is to build a strong and highly experienced community of freelancers and a thriving remote culture. We help businesses to get their work done at the most competitive price in a specific period offering a hassle-free payment and receiving method for both clients and freelancers.

Zillancer is the first freelancing platform in the middle east focusing on ERP and business management solutions. These categories are one of the least understood, exposed and full of complexity in the freelancing world. Many companies – large, medium and small – face challenges while finding the right solution to fit their business requirements. We see that as an opportunity: We are excited to simplify software solutions and digital services for everyone through our community.
