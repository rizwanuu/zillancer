Where does the name "Zillancer" come from?

Zillancer is a combination of zillions and freelancers. The founder Husain Abdulla aims to connect zillions of opportunities to zillions of talents and grow an expert community of freelancers worldwide. From the very beginning, Husain felt passionate about getting talented and skilled members to the platform, from a wide range of categories, including Microsoft Dynamics 365 consultants, SAP consultants, Oracle consultants, web developers, marketing specialists, graphic designers, and much more. Our freelancers have proven experience in their functional areas and can convey complex concepts to build your product faster with maximum efficiency.
